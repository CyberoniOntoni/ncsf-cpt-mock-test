Yes — completely free with no sign-up. Start practicing in your browser on any device.

150 questions randomly selected from a —-question pool, with shuffled answer options every retake.
What score do I need to pass?
70% — at least 105 correct out of 150. Your result is calculated instantly on submit.
How long do I have to complete the exam?
Each session has a 3-hour time limit. A timer runs during the exam. When time is up, the test auto-submits and shows whether you passed and how many questions were unanswered.
Will my progress survive a page refresh?
Yes — refreshing in the same browser tab restores your answers, question position, and remaining time. Progress clears when you submit, retake, or the 3-hour session expires.

Yes. The exam is optimized for mobile — tap answers, scroll explanations, and review results on a small screen.

No. This is a supplementary practice tool. Always use the official NCSF CPT textbook and resources for certification prep.
